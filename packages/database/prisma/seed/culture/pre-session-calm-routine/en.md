# A Pre‑Session Calm Ritual

**TL;DR:** the best freediving “hack” isn’t oxygen — it’s removing unnecessary tension.

## 60–120 seconds
- **Face:** relax forehead/jaw/tongue  
- **Shoulders:** drop and release  
- **Breathing:** 4 cycles: calm inhale → long exhale  
- **Anchor:** choose one focus (count, sound, air sensation)

## The common mistake
Trying to force calm. Calm emerges from softness and rhythm.

## Deeply format
- short audio cue
- face/shoulders/belly checklist
- “ready” → start

**Point:** quality begins before the attempt.
