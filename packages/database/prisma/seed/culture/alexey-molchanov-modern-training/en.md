# Alexey Molchanov: A Modern Approach

**TL;DR:** modern freediving isn’t “breath-hold magic” — it’s a **project**: periodization, technique, strength base, recovery, and psychology.

## What system-driven athletes usually do
- **Periodization:** accumulation → specificity → deload.
- **Technique as KPI:** equalization/movement quality beats one-off numbers.
- **Physical base:** legs, core, mobility, general endurance.
- **Recovery:** sleep, nutrition, stress management.

## Translating it to recreational training
1) 2–3 short sessions/week beat 1 heroic session  
2) Rate attempts by quality: “How calm was the middle?”  
3) Keep a log: sleep/stress/state → performance correlations

## Deeply building blocks
- Pre-session readiness (1–5)
- Post-session 3-question reflection
- Weekly plan: technique / easy volume / recovery

**Point:** performance is process management.
