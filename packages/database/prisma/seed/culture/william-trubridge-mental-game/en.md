# William Trubridge: Style & Mental Game

**TL;DR:** elite “calm” looks innate, but it’s a trained skill: managing attention, body tension, and rhythm.

## Three takeaways for anyone
1) **Rhythm beats speed**  
   Rushing often burns oxygen and focus.
2) **Focus on simple anchors**  
   Kick count, fin feel, relaxed face — small cues keep you stable.
3) **A scripted attempt**  
   Great dives have structure: start → cruise → turn → finish.

## A 2-minute Deeply drill
- 4 cycles: inhale — pause — long exhale  
- 10 seconds of stillness  
- cue: “slow and steady”

**Point:** calm is practice, not personality.
