# Mouthfill & Mobility: When “Air Runs Out”

**TL;DR:** as lungs compress at depth, equalization changes. Mouthfill is a way to pre‑load air for ears/mask without relying on chest pressure later.

## Why mobility matters
- ribcage/diaphragm must adapt to pressure,
- tension = higher cost and less control.

## Mouthfill in plain terms
- at a certain point, the diver “loads” air into mouth/throat,
- equalization then uses that reserve instead of chest pressure.

## Deeply framing (without risky how-to)
- explain the principle, not “exact meters”
- relaxation checklist: face/neck/shoulders
- mobility block: gentle stretching + breathing

**Note:** mouthfill is best learned with an instructor. Mistakes can lead to barotrauma.
