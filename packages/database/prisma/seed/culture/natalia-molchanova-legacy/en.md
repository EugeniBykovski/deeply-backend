# Natalia Molchanova: The Legacy

**TL;DR:** greatness is not only records — it’s the **quality of the method**: calm, efficiency, and recovery discipline.

## What defined her style
- **Efficiency:** minimal tension, maximum glide.
- **Consistency:** steady work over rare peaks.
- **State control:** training aligned with purpose, not “ego pushes.”

## Lessons for any level
1) Technique reduces cost (oxygen, nervous energy, mistakes).  
2) Consistency beats max attempts.  
3) Recovery is part of the plan — not an afterthought.

## Deeply ideas
- Pre-attempt checklist: relax jaw/shoulders/belly
- Track “quality” (1–5) instead of chasing depth
- Post-session ritual: 90 seconds of calm breathing

**Core point:** elite athletes aren’t “hard” — they’re **precise**.
