# Why You Shouldn’t Hyperventilate

**TL;DR:** hyperventilation lowers CO₂, not “loads you with oxygen.” You feel the urge to breathe later — and may push too far.

## The myth
“Breathing fast gives more oxygen.”  
In reality, most people are already near max oxygen saturation at rest.

## The reality
- CO₂ drops → urge to breathe arrives later
- higher chance of overextending
- especially dangerous in water

## What to do instead
- calm breathing
- long soft exhale
- relaxation and rhythm

## Deeply card
“3 calm cycles instead of 30 fast breaths” + one-paragraph explanation.

**Point:** safety starts with how you breathe before attempts.
