# Buddy Rule: A Checklist

**TL;DR:** safety is specific actions — not “we’re near each other.”

## What a buddy is
- someone who **watches you**, not someone also doing their own attempts;
- knows what to do and isn’t afraid to intervene.

## Mini checklist
- ✅ agree on the attempt plan (what/how long)  
- ✅ buddy watches the **end phase** and the surface  
- ✅ clear “I’m OK” signal  
- ✅ weird behavior → **contact and assist**

## The critical moment
The first seconds after surfacing. That’s where attention matters most.

## Deeply format
- before/during/after checklist
- “when to intervene” micro-cards
- reminder before in-water sessions

**Point:** a good buddy is a safety system.
