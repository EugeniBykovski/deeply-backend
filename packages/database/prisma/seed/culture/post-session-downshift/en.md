# Post‑Session Downshift

**TL;DR:** after effort (even mental), your body needs a “safe” signal. A short protocol helps normalize pulse and release leftover tension.

## 3–5 minute protocol
1) **Posture:** sit comfortably, drop shoulders  
2) **Breathing:** 6–8 long exhalations (exhale slightly longer than inhale)  
3) **Attention:** name 3 body sensations (warmth/cool/pressure)  
4) **Close:** one question: “What was the calmest moment?”

## Why it helps
- longer exhale supports downshifting,
- body attention reduces mental inertia.

## Deeply format
- 3‑minute timer
- cue: “exhale longer than inhale”
- one-line journal prompt

**Point:** recovery is a skill — it scales your progress.
