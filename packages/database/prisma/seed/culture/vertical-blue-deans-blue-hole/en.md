# Vertical Blue & Dean’s Blue Hole

**TL;DR:** iconic places produce iconic dives not by magic, but through a mix of conditions, logistics, and culture.

## Why Dean’s Blue Hole is famous
- **Depth nearby:** less travel offshore.
- **Relatively stable conditions:** easier planning.
- **Record history:** reputation boosts motivation and media attention.

## What makes Vertical Blue special
- A strong focus on depth disciplines and safety standards.
- Athletes and teams in one place → knowledge exchange.
- A season “anchor event” for many.

## Deeply angles
- “Places of depth”: a map of iconic freediving spots
- Why conditions matter: currents/temp/visibility and perceived effort
- Start rituals: how athletes prepare before attempts

**Point:** sometimes the biggest performance factor is the environment.
